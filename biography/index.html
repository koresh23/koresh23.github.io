    Biography

    Koresh Tony, as an artist, started his journey back in school - in 2008, he first tried himself as an MC, recording freestyles on a dictaphone. His friends supported Tony's venture, which contributed to his further development.

    2009: The first recorded tracks emerge.

    The performer starts his creative path under the alias Koresh MC.
    Participation in a live freestyle battle, where Tony confidently takes the 1st place.

    2010: In one of the online rap battles, Koresh takes the 3rd place among 16 participants.
    First live performance on the stage of his hometown.
    The same year, he participates in a large regional festival, representing his hometown on the stage in Koryazhma, Arkhangelsk Oblast, Russia.
    Tony actively performs on the stage of the college where he studies.
    Collaboration and track recordings with other local artists.

    2011-2012: Participation in two online rap battles. Tony consistently takes the 3rd place in both cases.
    The emergence of early popular tracks: "Dream", "Friday the 13th", "Resemblance of Socium", and others.

    2013: Tony records the first promotional tracks commissioned by a local entrepreneur for the "Vrubay" power tools store.
    Birth of his son.

    2014-2015: During this time, a small number of tracks were recorded. One notable example during this period is the track "Clone".
    In the autumn of 2015, Tony moves to Saint Petersburg.


    2016: The artist rebrands his work by changing his alias to Koresh Tony.
    Records the first track ("Mnogo Deneg") under the new pseudonym.

    2017: Creative pause.

    2018: Records the first track about cryptocurrency (CranePay).


    2019-2020: Creative pause.

    2021: Tony starts collaborating with the Dash cryptocurrency community.
    Records the track "Dash". Shoots the first music video (Koresh Tony - Dash).
    Records the first tracks in English ("Dash Jump", "Enemy in your TV", "Crypto and gun", and others).
    Collaborates with various cryptocurrency projects.

    2022: Releases the second music video (with the support of an investor from the Peercoin community): Koresh Tony - "Mnogo Deneg".
    New collaborations on a commercial basis.
    Birth of his daughter.

    2023: Release of the first two English music videos ("Ordinals Finance", "Quantum World").

    Currently shooting his first short film (with the support of an investor from the Quantum Resistant Ledger cryptocurrency community).
